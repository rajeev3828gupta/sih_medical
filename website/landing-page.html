Offline Concept

he app uses an Offline-First Architecture to overcome poor rural internet by prioritizing uninterrupted service. It achieves this by storing all essential data (profiles, prescriptions, stock) in encrypted local storage on the device.

When connectivity is available, the system uses Delta Sync (only uploading changes) and Conflict Resolution to efficiently update the central server.

Core features like telemedicine (using Store-and-Forward media) and the Offline AI Symptom Checker are designed to function fully without a network, ensuring healthcare access is maintained even in 2G or no-network areas.
